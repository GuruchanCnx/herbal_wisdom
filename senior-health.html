Senior Health & Herbal Support

Traditional Plants for Senior Wellness

Northern Thai medicinal plants offer gentle, effective support for common senior health concerns. These time-tested remedies have been used for generations to maintain vitality and wellness in elders.

Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

For: Joint pain, inflammation, liver health

How to use: Golden milk tea, cooking spice

Caution: May interact with blood thinners

Gotu Kola (Centella asiatica)

For: Memory enhancement, wound healing, ulcers

How to use: Fresh leaves in salads, tea

Caution: Safe for most seniors

Moringa (Moringa oleifera)

For: Malnutrition, diabetes management, immune support

How to use: Powder in smoothies, fresh leaves in soups

Caution: Monitor blood sugar if diabetic
